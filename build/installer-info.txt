Self-hosted bot by Core

This installer sets up the desktop control panel and bundled runtime for the
self-hosted Discord bot.

What is included:
- Desktop control panel for token, server, channel, command, and warning settings.
- Local SQLite storage, no MongoDB requirement.
- Start, restart, stop, emergency stop, and live log view.
- Guild command registration and moderation tools.

After installation:
1. Open Self-hosted bot by Core.
2. Paste your Discord bot token, bot ID, server ID, and log channel IDs.
3. Press Save.
4. Press Start.
